Core Components

💡

Photoresistor (LDR)

Resistance sharply decreases when bombarded by photons. Perfect for ambient light-sensing applications.

R = f(Lux-γ)
⚡

Photodiode

Immediately converts light pulses into electrical current. Extremely fast response time; standard for optical fiber links.

~
🔋

Phototransistor

Combines light-sensing with internal current amplification. Highly sensitive, capable of multiplying tiny inputs.
